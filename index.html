About Me

"Wake up determined. Go to bed satisfied. And somewhere in between eat a cookie.." - Dwayne Johnson

I graduated from the University of California, Irvine with a Bachelors in Mathematics in 2015 and Codesmith in Immersive Full Stack Javascript in 2016.  

Since then, I’ve learnt a great deal from interacting with people in and out of the engineering department to working on some projects after hours, from learning new technologies to explaining coding concepts to friends who want to get into and might already be in coding bootcamps or related fields.   While knowing everything can definitely have its benefits, so can having an eternal hunger and thirst to learn more.

I'm a driven person, motivated by knowledge and the unknown.  If I encounter a problem that I cannot solve, I'll find a solution - then optimize that path to the solution or the solution itself.   If my passion and drive were a superhero, it would be like the Hulk, not the green part but the size and growth part.
